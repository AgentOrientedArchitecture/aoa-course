Frontend Engineer — Design Systems
Marrowbone Studio (Remote, UK time zones)

We make design tools for technical teams. Our flagship is a collaborative
diagramming app used by a few thousand engineering teams. We're hiring a
frontend engineer to come and own our design system.

The work
- Take ownership of our internal component library: a Tailwind + Radix
  setup with around 80 components.
- Drive the migration of two remaining product surfaces (admin and billing)
  off the legacy Bootstrap stack onto the design system.
- Be the engineer designers come to when they want to know "can we do this".
- Own visual regression: build out the Storybook + Playwright pipeline.

Must have
- 3+ years professional frontend experience, primarily in React + TypeScript.
- Track record of working closely with product designers.
- Comfortable in CSS — you can read a Figma file and reproduce it without
  fighting the layout system.
- Experience with Storybook or a similar component-isolation tool.

Nice to have
- Have shipped a design system from scratch, or migrated one.
- Open-source design-system or UI primitives work in your portfolio.
- Comfortable writing and maintaining accessibility tests.

Salary £70-85k. Fully remote, UK time zone. We meet up in person once a
quarter, location rotates.
