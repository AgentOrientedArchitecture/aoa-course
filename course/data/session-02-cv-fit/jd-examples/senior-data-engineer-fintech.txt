Senior Data Engineer — Healthcare Payments
Hexley & Park (London / hybrid, 2 days in office)

About us
Hexley & Park is the payments platform behind several large NHS trusts and
private healthcare networks. We move regulated money in regulated places,
which means our data platform is held to a higher bar than most.

The role
We're hiring a senior data engineer to take ownership of our analytics
warehouse and the contracts around it. You'll work with product engineers,
finance, and our compliance team. Reporting to our Head of Data.

What you'll do
- Own our Snowflake + dbt warehouse end to end: modelling, tests, lineage,
  the lot.
- Define data contracts between product and analytics so schema changes
  don't break dashboards on Monday morning.
- Lead a small team (currently two; growing to four next year).
- Be on the on-call rota with the rest of the data team (1 week in 5).
- Work with our compliance lead on data classification and retention so we
  stay on the right side of FCA and ICO expectations.

What you'll bring
Essential
- 5+ years building production data pipelines in a regulated environment
  (financial services, healthcare, insurance — something where audit
  matters).
- Deep Python and SQL.
- Strong dbt + Snowflake (or close cousin like BigQuery + dbt) experience.
- Track record of leading a small team through delivery.
- Comfortable being on call for the data platform.

Nice to have
- Direct experience with NHS or healthcare data.
- Experience of GDPR / DPA in practice (DPIAs, retention schedules).
- Have shipped data contracts before — not just talked about them.
- Open-source contributions to the data tooling ecosystem.

What we offer
Salary £85-105k depending on experience. Share options. 28 days holiday plus
bank holidays. Hybrid: two days a week in the King's Cross office, three
days from wherever you work best.
